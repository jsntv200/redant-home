Cyber Security

We look at Cyber Security from a Development perspective

Many security teams will start with infrastructure and networks. Probing to see how a bad actor can get in to your platform.

Our take is a bit different.

We think you’re just as likely to be exposed by poor software - either in how it was built or is being maintained.
