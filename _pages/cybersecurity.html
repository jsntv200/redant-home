We look at CYBER
SECURITY from a DEVELOPMENT
PERSPECTIVE

Many security teams will start with infrastructure and networks.

Probing to see how a bad actor can get in to vour platform.

Our take is a bit different.

We think you're just as likely to be exposed by poor software - either in how it was built or is being maintained.

That open door in your software might be easier to slip through than dropping ninja style into your data centre.

How good is the actual code that runs your site or platform? How well was it developed?

Most software has some level of bugs, but is yours more or less than typical?

Have you picked the right frameworks and tech stack?

Are there bits lying around which could be exploited?

Are there steps in your process which exposes sensitive data?

If someone did gain access, what kind of things could they do?

If something does go seriously wrong, can the system be quickly rolled back?

We look at your systems from a developer perspective - your team, processes and technology. Unlike the traditional approach. which tvoically starts from a System Interation and Network view we start with vour code How it was made How vou test and deploy changes. How you process data.

Even the most bullet proof network and infrastructure can be undone by poor development practices/process/ream.
